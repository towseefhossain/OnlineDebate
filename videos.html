2017 - Training Videos

Digital Bitorko Forum 1.0 is the first initiative of its kind by members of the Bangladesh Debating Community , in an attempt to expand access to debate training, workshops, content and resources through an accessible online forum.
Digital Bitorko Forum is run by the admins of Online Debates, who not only aim to facilitate the proliferation of debate content and increase its accessibility but also seek to provide a digital platform for debate practice and for debaters and adjudicators from all over the country to connect.

February 2019 Technolog.IV 2019

Technolog.IV 2019 is our first inter-varsity debating competition that will entertain teams and adjudicators from all over the world. With a keen eye towards giving a platform for budding university debaters, whether they be veterans or novice ones, Technolog.IV promises to have some brilliant debates, quality motions, a top-notch digital break, an outstanding Core Adjudication Panel and a chance to debate, regardless of where you live, free and from the comfort of your own homes!

December 2018 Technologics 2018

We give to you, TechnoLogics 2018! It's our utmost pleasure to bring to you the digital debate revolution that aspires to shred boundaries, connect communities and bring debating to the comfort of your own homes.
Gear up for some spectacular debates, high quality motions and top notch adjudication all while you get to enjoy lunch and dinner with your family who's probably been complaining that you've been taking too many weekends off for debates. Combined with what is probably the world's first ever digital break night, trust us when we say that we have much more in store for you!
