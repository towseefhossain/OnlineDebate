About Us

Online Debates is a digital debate community that was originally founded in Bangladesh by a few high school and university kids to host practice debates online. Now, the community features regular free digital debate tournaments, frequent weekend debates and an assortment of training materials and live interactive workshops in an attempt to make debating as accessible as ever.
